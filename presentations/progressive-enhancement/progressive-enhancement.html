Graceful Degradation

You focus in building sites so they work in the most advanced/capable browsers then you add handlers for the less capable ones.

Graceful Degradation

It is expected that the user experience in less capable browsers to be poor but aceptable.

Progressive Enhancement

On the other hand, progressive enhancement focuses on the content, not the browsers.

So what's the difference?

  • It’s a matter of perspective.
  • Both consider how well a site works in a variety of browsers on a variety of devices.
  • The key is where they place their focus and how this affects workflow.

Conclusions

  • Focus on the content
  • Give the users the best possible experience given the device capabilities
  • Don't spend your time trying to make old browsers do what they are not meant to do
  • Give users a reason to upgrade
